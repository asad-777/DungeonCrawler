# ⚔️ Dungeon Crawler: Explore. Battle. Survive.

Welcome to **Dungeon Crawler**, a grid-based adventure where strategy meets survival. Navigate through dangerous corridors, avoid deadly traps, and face off against monstrous foes to claim the ultimate prize.

This game was built for **[Hack Club Campfire](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://hackclub.com/campfire/)** and marks a major milestone: **the first game I have ever built!**

## 🎮 Gameplay

You find yourself at the top of a treacherous dungeon. Your goal is simple but the execution is anything but:

* **Navigate the Maze:** Use your wits to find the path to the treasure chest.
* **Enemies & Traps:** Avoid the watchful eyes of the Beholders, the strength of the Ogres, and the hidden floor spikes.
* **The Goal:** Reach the chest at the bottom right to secure your loot and escape!
